Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This app allows users to post Amazon product links to their Pinterest boards. It is a personal tool and does not collect, store, or share any personal data beyond what is required to authenticate with Pinterest.

Data We Access

We access your Pinterest account only to post pins on your behalf. We do not store your Pinterest credentials. Access tokens are stored temporarily in your session and expire automatically.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the Pinterest API and the Anthropic Claude API to extract product information. No personal data is shared with these services beyond what is necessary to complete the requested action.

Cookies

This app uses a session cookie solely to maintain your authenticated state. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Contact

For any questions, contact the app owner directly.
